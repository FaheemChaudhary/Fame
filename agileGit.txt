What are Agile ceremonies?
Scrum ceremonies are important elements of the agile software delivery process. 
 these scrum ceremonies provide the framework for teams to get work done in a 
structured manner, help to set expectations, empower the team to collaborate effectively,
 and ultimately drive results. 
 The four scrum ceremonies are:
1.Sprint Planning
2.Daily Scrum
3.Sprint Review
4.Sprint Retrospective
Scrum is a way to implement agile.
